About romitagl.com

Romitagl.com is my personal website and platform dedicated to providing free, privacy-focused web tools and resources. My mission is to support developers, businesses, and everyday users by offering utilities that simplify common tasks and enhance productivity - all without compromising privacy.

I believe in the power of open-source and community-driven projects, which is why I share my tools and projects openly on GitHub.

Our Tools Philosophy

Privacy First

All our tools perform processing directly in your browser. Your files and data never leave your device, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

Open Source

Our code is transparent and available for inspection on GitHub. You can verify exactly how your data is being processed or even contribute improvements.

Free Forever

All tools are completely free to use without any hidden costs, premium features, or usage limitations. No account required, no strings attached.
